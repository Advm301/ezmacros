Signing in

QuickPrep uses passwordless sign-in — enter your email and you'll get a one-time code or magic link, no password to remember or reset.

Deleting your account

Open the app, and near the "Sign Out" button you'll find "Delete Account." This permanently removes your diary, ratings, photos, and account. It can't be undone.
