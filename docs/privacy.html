QuickPrep Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

QuickPrep is a recipe and meal-planning app. This page explains what data the app collects, how it's used, and how to have it deleted.

What we collect

  • Email address — used only to sign you in via a one-time code or magic link. We don't use passwords, so there's nothing else to store on that front.
  • Diary entries — the meals you plan for a given day (recipe and date), so the app can show you your own meal plan.
  • Recipe ratings — the star rating you give a recipe, and the date you rated it.
  • Photos (optional) — if you choose to attach a photo when rating a recipe, it's uploaded and stored so you can see it later.

Recipes you star as "Saved," and any notes or ingredient edits you make to a recipe, are stored only on your own device (in local browser storage) and are never sent to our servers.

How it's used

All of the above exists solely to power the app's own features: showing your diary, your ratings, and your saved recipes back to you. We don't run ads, sell data, or share it with third parties. There's no analytics or tracking SDK in the app.

What's visible to other users

Your star rating contributes to a recipe's public average rating (shown as a number, e.g. "4.5 ★"), the same way it would in any recipe app. Individual ratings and any photos you upload are private — visible only to you, never to other users.

Where data lives

Data is stored with Supabase (hosted in the United States). Photos are stored in a private bucket and only ever served to you via short-lived signed links.

Deleting your data

You can permanently delete your account at any time from inside the app (look for "Delete Account" near the sign-out button). This immediately and permanently removes your diary entries, ratings, uploaded photos, and account from our servers. It can't be undone. Locally saved recipes and notes are cleared from your device at the same time.

Children

QuickPrep is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change with it.
